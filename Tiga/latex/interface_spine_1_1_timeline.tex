Sets the value(s) for the specified time. 


\begin{DoxyParams}{매개변수}
{\em skeleton} & The skeleton the timeline is being applied to. This provides access to the bones, slots, and other skeleton components the timeline may change.\\
\hline
{\em last\+Time} & last\+Time The time this timeline was last applied. Timelines such as \mbox{\hyperlink{class_spine_1_1_event_timeline}{Event\+Timeline}} trigger only at specific times rather than every frame. In that case, the timeline triggers everything between last\+Time (exclusive) and 
\begin{DoxyCode}{1}
\DoxyCodeLine{time}
\end{DoxyCode}
 (inclusive).\\
\hline
{\em time} & The time within the animation. Most timelines find the key before and the key after this time so they can interpolate between the keys.\\
\hline
{\em events} & If any events are fired, they are added to this list. Can be null to ignore firing events or if the timeline does not fire events. May be null.\\
\hline
{\em alpha} & alpha 0 applies the current or setup pose value (depending on pose parameter). 1 applies the timeline value. Between 0 and 1 applies a value between the current or setup pose and the timeline value. By adjusting alpha over time, an animation can be mixed in or out. 
\begin{DoxyCode}{1}
\DoxyCodeLine{alpha}
\end{DoxyCode}
 can also be useful to apply animations on top of each other (layered).\\
\hline
{\em pose} & Controls how mixing is applied when alpha is than 1.\\
\hline
{\em direction} & Indicates whether the timeline is mixing in or out. Used by timelines which perform instant transitions such as \mbox{\hyperlink{class_spine_1_1_draw_order_timeline}{Draw\+Order\+Timeline}} and \mbox{\hyperlink{class_spine_1_1_attachment_timeline}{Attachment\+Timeline}}.\\
\hline
